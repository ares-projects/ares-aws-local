# Ares AWS Local commit convention

## Format

`<type>(<scope>): <description>`

Allowed types: `feat`, `fix`, `refactor`, `perf`, `test`, `docs`, `build`, `ci`, `chore`, `revert`.

Allowed scopes: `local`, `gradle`, `build`, `ci`, `docs`, `repo`, `test`.

Subjects use imperative mood, start lowercase, have no final period, and are at most 72 characters. Bodies are optional and wrap at 100 characters. Do not mention Claude, Codex, AI, prompts, or generated code. AI attribution and `Co-authored-by` trailers are prohibited.
